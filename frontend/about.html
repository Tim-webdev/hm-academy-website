About Us

Located in Navy Estate, Karshi, Abuja, Hands and Minds Computer Academy is a premier training institute dedicated to equipping individuals with practical, job-ready digital skills.

🌟 Our Mission

To empower individuals with cutting-edge digital skills and technology education that prepares them for global opportunities and careers.

👁️ Our Vision

To be a leading center of excellence in digital education, nurturing innovation and self-reliance in Nigeria and beyond.

📜 Our Background

Founded by passionate tech educators, Hands and Minds Computer Academy was established to bridge the digital skill gap in Abuja. With a strong focus on practical learning, we offer training in fields such as Web Development, Graphic Design, UI/UX Design, Office Tools, and more.
